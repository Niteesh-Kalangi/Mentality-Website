Exercise and Nutrition Introduction

Keeping your body in good shape is essential for a good mental state. As a result, exercise and diet can play large roles in your mental health. In this section, we detail the importance of each and a basic explanation of how to improve both facets of your health. Your mind depends on your body as much as your body depends on your mind. By keeping your body healthy, your mind will automatically start to become healthier.
