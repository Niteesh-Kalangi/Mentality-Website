Calming Videos

This video displays a rainforest, the events happening there, and all the sounds. It is great to enhance your spiritual side, feel in touch with the earth, and in doing so, become less stressed.
This video displays movements in outer space, along with calming background music. It can help you gain perspective that the universe is vast. Us humans and our lives make up an infinitely small percent of what happens. Just know this whenever a momentous or important event is happening.
This video captures the sky and the movements in it. The swaying of the clouds and the upward view of the world can remind us that the sky is the limit for our potential. Any stress over obstacles you are facing now are temporary, and the sky is the limit for you.
This video captures the beachfront. The ocean waves in the calm environment of the beach should convince you to relax. You may feel overwhelmed with life and work, but just know that you can escape your troubles. Look forward to a time where you will be on a beach, relaxing as the ocean catches your gaze.
